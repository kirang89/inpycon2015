Nicholas H.Tollervey

Nicholas is software engineer, classically trained musician, philosophy graduate, teacher and writer. He has authored Python in Education , co-authored Learning jQuery Deferreds and Getting Started with Fluidinfo . His current passion is the drogulus, a programmable peer-to-peer data store that was hacked together in free time. He is a fellow at PSF. He is one of organizers of PyCon UK. He goes by the handle ntoll in twitter.
